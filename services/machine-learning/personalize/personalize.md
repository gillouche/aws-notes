# Amazon Personalize

## General info

recommender systems - create individualized recommendations for customers using their apps

collaborative filtering engine

used for personalized product and content recommendations, tailored search results, targeted marketing promotions

need to provide an activity stream for the app such as page views, signups, purchases as well as an inventory of the items we want to recommend
such as articles, products, videos or music

we can also choose to provide Personalize with additional demographic information from your users such as age, geo location, ...

algo automatically selected for training and optimization

data is kept private and secure and only used for customized recommendations
